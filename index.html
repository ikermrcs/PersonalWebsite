ABOUT ME

Hi! My name is Iker and I am currently in my final years of Law, Business Administration and Finance, about to begin my professional career.

Although I had already planned to dedicate myself to the world of investment before, during this time I have been able to learn a lot more and have a clearer idea. My main interest goes beyond traditional investment. What I really like are hedging strategies, riskier assets such as options and futures. Within this interest I also include for me the commodity sector, the Atlas of our economy, led by the "the endless trains of the faithless".

As for my qualities I consider these to be some of the main ones in me:

  • Critcal. I am able to analyse situations from various perspectives and in an objective manner, which allows me to make better decisions.
  • Resolute. I remain calm in any situation, no matter how high the pressure may be, and I seek solutions to problems quickly and decisively.
  • Creative. I look for original solutions, facing challenges in unusual ways, and I explore new ideas and approaches.
  • Versatile. I adapt easily to any environment or situation, being able to take on different roles, and I have a facility for learning new concepts.

In short, I am a person who is always eager to learn something new and grow as a person. I love challenges and facing them in the most creative way, and I always try to leave a positive footprint and contribute positively to my environment.

I look forward to the challenges this journey will present, and I think the most exciting thing about it is the chance to contribute a verse to this powerful play!

EDUCATIONAL PATHWAY

"An investment in knowledge pays the best interest."

- Benjamin Franklin

2005

In 2005 I was admitted to Sacred Heart Carmelite School, where I spent 15 years of my life. During these years I was a member of the school's football team, I participated in numerous competitions such as the maths olympiads or writing competitions, and I also studied, in a complementary manner, professional music studies. In this same school I studied my baccalaureate in social sciences, beginning my preparation to access the double degree of Business Administration and Law.

2020

Once I finished my baccalaureate studies, I was admitted to the University of Deusto to study a double degree in Business Administration and Law, being Deusto considered one of the best universities in Spain to study these degrees. I am currently in my 4th year focusing on my professional career which will start soon.

2021

While studying at Deusto I realised that I had a great interest in finance; I was sure that in the future I would work in something related to it. Therefore, to perfect my knowledge acquired at Deusto and because of the strong interest I had in finance, I started an online degree through the University of La Rioja.

2022

In 2022 I had the opportunity to spend the summer doing a course on financial risk analysis and management at the London School of Economics. It was undoubtedly one of the best experiences of my life: I developed my knowledge on a subject of great interest to me, such as options and futures, I met many people and made some incredible friends, and I enjoyed college life at one of the world's leading universities.

EXTRACURRICULAR ACTIVITIES

"Leyendo en Clave Jurídica"

I am a member of the Deusto Investment Club, the largest in Spain with more than €90,000 of AuM. Participating in it has allowed me to attend Bloomberg and Valuation sessions given by professionals, and to weekly club sessions that are developed with the same methodology as an investment fund.

"Leyendo en Clave Jurídica" is a reading club organised by the Faculty of Law of Deusto. As a member of the club, I read a book of my choice from among four, as do the other members, and once a term we meet to discuss what we thought of it and reflect on it.

I am currently part of the Berakah Programme, which aims to help the underprivileged who have been left behind by the system. I am currently involved in teaching small groups of primary school students to help them in their studies.
